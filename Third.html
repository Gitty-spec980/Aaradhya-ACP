Hello there! Welcome to the Hone Blog. You are officially our first blogger to enter the website.

To start off, honey bees are social insects known for producing honey and pollinating plants. They live in colonies with a complex social structure, including a queen, worker bees, and drones. Honey bees are not native to the United States but were introduced from Europe and Africa.

Fun fact! Did you know that a honey bee can fly up to 15 miles per hour? A honey bee worker only makes an average of 1/12 of a teaspoon in her lifetime. One ounce of honey would fuel a bees flight around the world. A pound honey is made by 2 million flower visits.
